About Panaache Pageant

Panaache Miss & Mrs India International is a celebration of women empowerment and emancipation through a platform where they can showcase their talent to the world. This pageant invites women from all walks of life who have dared to drea big, to present themselves on a global stage. At Panaache, we inspire and motivate women to come forward and create a niche for themselves and spread the message of true essence of being a woman. We welcome you to take the first step and begin a journey with us from Queen of the Home to Queen of the World!!!

About Our Director

Meet Kanika Baheti – The Visionary Behind Panaache Kanika Baheti is a supermodel, fashion designer, and entrepreneur, leading Panaache with style and innovation. She has walked the ramp at Lakmé Fashion Week, graced the cover of a London magazine, and continues to make waves in the fashion industry. With an eye for elegance and a passion for design, Kanika blends creativity with business, shaping a brand that celebrates confidence, beauty, and excellence.
